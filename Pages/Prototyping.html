Prototyping

The Adobe Flash Professional CC 2015 was used to create the prototype of the multimedia product so as the reason why I selected that was to make it user-friendly.

All the other designs were also developed and edited using the Adobe Flash Professional CC 2015 and exported them into prototypes. The final product was launched by improving the prototypes. In order to run prototypes in a web browser, I exported it into SWF format. Therefore, it can be opened and used on a flash player.

I installed the latest version of the Adobe Flash player into my computer to present this product to our client, Mr. Dinesh Balasooriya. I have tested the product and also collected user feedbacks to make further modifications to the product.

The third meeting was started at 3.00 p.m. on 22nd August 2018 and held in the school main hall. All the decisions were taken in the last meeting regarding the designs of the product were briefed by me. After that, I presented the prototype to them.

I also showed the prototype to Mr. Dinesh Balasooriya & Mr. Chamal Rajapaksha with the use of my laptop. I guided them through the prototype at first as they watched. Only links that working were the few pages of the Notes section, Home and Activities buttons.  Each of them went through the prototype on their own and gave the suggestions to modify the product.

The given suggestions were listed below:

  • Background colour changes to the product
  • Add more multimedia components to the product
  • Add a few buttons and make few changes to the included multimedia components on the home page.

Mr. Dinesh Balasooriya and Mr. Chamal Rajapaksha were satisfied with the proposed prototype since it had user friendly look. They told me to developed a second prototype with the improvements and present it on upcoming week.  The next meeting is scheduled to be on 29th August 2018 at the same place.

on 29th August 2018, the fourth meeting was held at the school main hall. We commenced the meeting at 1.05 p.m. because Mr Dinesh Balasooriya came a bit late. The meeting was held to present 2nd after further improvements from the 1st one. I showed the enhanced prototype to Mr Dinesh Balasooriya & Mr Chamal Rajapaksha pointed out the changes that I had made. The working links at the occasion are Question, short note, home button and one page of a short note.

Mr Chamal Rajapaksha said that the product was done well and have an attractive look compared to the first one, but there are few improvements to be done still. Thereafter he told to add a few images and videos to make the product more attractive and interactive. After that Mr Dinesh Balasooriya told product colour could be changed to a shade of green since the current colour is very colourful and bit hard to work with it for many hours. He satisfied with the few buttons I have added to the product. However, he was not fully happy with the layout thereby he gave me a few suggestions to be done to the product to make it more effective.

However, Mr. Chamal Rajapaksha and Mr. Dinesh Balasooriya were satisfied with the second prototype than the first one.

They requested me to complete the final product as soon as possible. After that discussion was made about the sitemap for the ebook. During the discussion, they told me to present the sitemap within a few days to get an idea of how the information would be: separated into subheadings and separate fields and how they will be connected.

The next meeting was scheduled to be on 2nd September 2018 at the same place. Meeting No.4  Adjourned at  3.00 p.m.
