User Feedback

User feedbacks were collected: to ensure that the product meets the client's requirements fully. The final multimedia product was integrated: into DVDs alongside its user guide. in order to collect user feedbacks, Final product and a questionary were distributed: among three test users. I gave one day to all the test users to fill the questionnaire with the use of the multimedia product. Also, I told them to hand over the questionaries once they filled them within the given time.

Once I received the questionaries, I analyzed all the questionaries one by one and made amended to the multimedia product to accomplish the requirements of the user and client's.

Three types of user feedback were gathered: and they were listed as follows

  • Given user feedbacks for the prototype 1
  • Given user feedbacks for the prototype 2
  • Given user feedbacks for the final multimedia product
