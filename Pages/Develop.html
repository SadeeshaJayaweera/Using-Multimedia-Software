Development

Now it’s the time to produce a fully working multimedia product by refining after completion of the second prototype. Also feedbacks, comments and suggestions were taken from the client and taken them into account, during this process.  We have also conducted a frequent meeting with the members of the advanced level section of the Scholl and the Client. Thereby they will be up to date with the progress of the multimedia product.

The development of the multimedia product done by using the Adobe Flash Professional CC 2015.  The main reason to choose this software because it’s very user-friendly and allowed me to add many multimedia components into both prototypes and the final product such as images, text, videos, etc.  

The information added to this eBook “Your Pal in GIT” was taken by many books and also from the internet. Most of the content was taken: from the “www.apepanthiya.com”.

The questions were taken from many websites such as “www.nie.lk.”, “www.guruwaraya.lk” and images were downloaded from the google also some were made by me. I used Microsoft office 2016 and Adobe Photoshop CC 2015 to edit and create images. The downloaded videos were edited using the software called total video converter. The multimedia product was tested once it was completed also user feedback was gathered to find issues and improvements needed to be done to the product. Thereby we made sure that all the client’s requirements were met.

After user feedback was received, a modified multimedia product was integrated: into DVD disk drive with its user guide. The DVD disk drive which contains multimedia product in SWF format and user guide in txt format was ready to be delivered to Mr Dinesh Bamunusingha.
